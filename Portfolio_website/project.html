MY PROJECTS

E-ATTENDANCE THROUGH FACE RECOGNITION

An automated attendance system leveraging facial recognition technology is implemented through a webcam capturing students' or employees' images. These images are then cross-referenced with a database containing registered faces. When a match is found, the system marks the individual's attendance. This innovative solution ensures accurate and efficient attendance tracking, saving time and effort for educational institutions or businesses. By eliminating manual processes, the system streamlines administrative tasks and enhances overall security. Moreover, it fosters a seamless experience for students or employees while promoting the adoption of cutting-edge technology in educational and organizational settings.

PROSHOP

Proshop is a dynamic e-commerce platform enabling customers to not only purchase products but also leverage it as a virtual marketplace to sell their own goods and offer services. This innovative website fosters a two-way exchange, empowering users to engage in both buying and selling activities with ease. Through a user-friendly interface, sellers can list their products or services, while buyers can browse through a diverse range of offerings. Proshop creates an inclusive and interactive online community, fostering entrepreneurship and facilitating seamless transactions for customers seeking unique products and services in the digital marketplace.

BERTFLIX

BertFlix, an ambitious open-source movie application, strives to provide users with a feature-rich experience for exploring and engaging with an extensive library of movies and TV Serials. This comprehensive project aims to empower users with seamless browsing, efficient search functionalities, and interactive features. By leveraging open-source principles, BertFlix seeks to foster a community-driven platform that continuously evolves to meet the entertainment needs of its users. With an ever-expanding collection of films and TV shows, this innovative application promises to revolutionize the way audiences enjoy and appreciate cinematic experiences.
